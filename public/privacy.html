Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OFX Converter ("we", "our", or "the service") handles information when you use our free bank statement conversion tool at ofx-converter.netlify.app.

Data we do not collect

OFX Converter processes all files entirely in your browser. Your bank statements, financial data, and converted OFX files are never sent to any server. We have no access to the contents of any file you upload.

Data collected automatically

When you visit this site, our hosting provider (Netlify) may collect standard server log data, including:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring URL

This data is collected by Netlify for operational purposes and is governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

OFX Converter itself does not set any cookies. Third-party services (Netlify, Google AdSense) may set cookies subject to their own policies.

Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

Contact
